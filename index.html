I am a research fellow in the College of Computing and Data Science (CCDS), Nanyang Technological University. I received my Ph.D. in Intelligent Science (2023), B.Sc. in Computer Science (2018), and B.Ec in Economics (2018) degrees from Peking University. My research focuses on AI-driven data visualization and human–computer interaction, with particular emphasis on how AI can support human understanding of complex data. Specifically, I am interested in intelligent data interaction, intelligent visual design, and intelligent data management. I also explore how AI enables more creative visual representations and supports applications in digital humanities.

I enjoy writing Chinese poetry and hiking, often at the same time. Visit Mushan Collection to read some of my ancient-style poems.
