I received my Ph.D. Degree from the School of Intelligence Science and Technology, Peking University in 2023. I received my Bachelor of Science from the School of EECS and Bachelor of Economics from the National School of Development at Peking University in 2018. My research interest lies in deep learning-driven data visualization and human-computer interaction.

I enjoy writing Chinese poetry and hiking, often at the same time. Visit this page to read some of my poems.
